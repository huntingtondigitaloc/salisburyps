What is the Clarisonic® Skin Care System?

The Clarisonic® skin care system is a gentle sonic micro-massage that promotes cleaner, healthier skin.

Benefits of the Clarisonic® Skin Care System

The Clarisonic® skin care system is highly recommended by skin care specialists around the world for its effectiveness in cleansing skin, with the capacity to remove up to six times more makeup residue than regular cleansing. Cleansing regularly with the Clarisonic® skin care system helps reduce oily areas, dry skin patches, and blemishes. Regular use of the system also helps reduce the appearance of visible pores, fine lines, and wrinkles.

Why Choose the Clarisonic® Skin Care System?

Effective cleansing enhances your appearance by presenting fresher, cleaner skin. The Clarisonic® skin care system cleanses so well that it helps your skin absorb all products better so you get more benefits from all of your skin care products.

How Often Can I Use the Clarisonic® Skin Care System?

The Clarisonic® skin care system is gentle enough to be used twice daily.
If you want to ensure that you’re cleansing your skin thoroughly and eliminating all makeup residue, oil, and dead skin cells, the Clarisonic® skin care system may be ideal for you.
