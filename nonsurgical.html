BioTE®

BioTE is an implanted hormone therapy pellet, which is formulated and personalized for each patient that slowly releases a customized BHRT into the bloodstream. Men and women struggling with unbalanced hormones or who experience symptoms, such as weight gain, low libido, mood swings, and fatigue, could benefit from this new and simplified HRT process.

HydraFacial®

PlasmaPen®

PlasmaPen® is a revolutionary new concept in non-invasive skin lifting and tightening using plasma energy. This transmission of energy from the probe on the tip of the pen to the skin’s epidermal layer creates an immediate lifting and tightening of the skin.

SkinPen®

The Skinpen® is the first FDA cleared micro-needling device to treat skin imperfections such as scarring, pigmentation, age spots, fine lines, and wrinkles and stretch marks. Skinpen® also boosts collagen production, resulting in firmer and smoother skin. Salisbury Plastic Surgery, led by board-certified plastic surgeon Dr. Deborah K. Ekstrom, provides effective treatment with Skinpen® for individuals in Shrewsbury, Worcester, and neighboring communities in Massachusetts, who want to improve their facial appearance.

Microneedling With Radiofrequency

Microneedling with radiofrequency, like other microneedling procedures, creates controlled micro-injuries in the skin to stimulate collagen production. Other methods of microneedling utilize PRP, stem cell growth factor, and other serums to penetrate deep into the skin and further enhance results.

Morpheus8

Morpheus 8 is a safe and effective minimally invasive modular RF fractional solution for facial and body subdermal adipose remodeling. At Salisbury Plastic Surgery we strive to bring the most innovative safe, and effective non-surgical treatments and technologies to our patients. InMode’s Morpheus 8 combines Radio Frequency and Microneedling to create tiny puncture wounds to the superficial dermis to stimulate collagen, elastin, and new skin cells Morpheus 8 delivers the deepest fractional treatments available, penetrating subdermal tissue up to 8 mm.
