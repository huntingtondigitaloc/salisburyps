ABOUT Laser Genesis

Laser Genesis treatment uses laser technology to safely, effectively and discretely and effectively diminish the appearance of fine line wrinkles, large pores, uneven skin texture, discoloration and scars on the skin. This advanced treatment is now available at Salisbury Plastic Surgery, led by board-certified plastic surgeon Dr. Deborah K. Ekstrom, serving residents of Shrewsbury, Worcester, and surrounding areas in Massachusetts.

How Does Laser Genesis Work?

The Laser Genesis is a non-ablative, non-invasive form of skin therapy that utilizes state-of-the-art laser technology to safely and effectively treat uneven skin texture, scars, large pores, and discoloration. Laser Genesis works by gently heating the upper dermis well below your skin’s surface to stimulate production of collagen, a substance that fortifies and strengthens skin cells at the structural level.

How Can Laser Genesis Improve My Appearance?

Laser Genesis patients report a high degree of satisfaction with their treatment results and experience a dramatic reduction in surface skin imperfections, such as scars and discoloration.

What Can I Expect from Laser Genesis?

Patients find the Laser Genesis treatment painless, relaxing and therapeutic. You will experience a warming sensation in your skin as the laser device is moved back and forth about a half inch from the surface of your skin. You will be able to immediately return to normal non-strenuous activities, but you must protect your skin with protective clothing or sunscreen outdoors. You will see subtle yet consistent results following each treatment, with no bruising or excessive skin irritation.

How many Laser Genesis treatments will I need?

The treatment results from Laser Genesis are subtle and most patients require four to six treatments in order to achieve optimal results. Every patient’s condition is unique, and Laser Genesis may be combined with other procedures to produce a more dramatic improvement.

What is the Cost of Laser Genesis in Worcester?

Your Laser Genesis treatment will be priced according to the extent of your treatment and the number of sessions you require. At Salisbury Plastic Surgery, you can use various payment methods and we offer flexible financing options for your ease and convenience.
