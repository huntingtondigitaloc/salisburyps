Meet The Staff of Salisbury Plastic Surgery

Dr. Deborah K. Ekstrom

Double Board Certified Plastic Surgeon, Dr. Deborah K. Ekstrom, is the Medical Director and Owner of Salisbury Plastic Surgery in Worcester, MA.

Dr. Deborah K. Ekstrom is a native of the Midwestern US and has adopted New England as her home for the past 30 years.

Dr. Ekstrom is an experienced, board-certified plastic surgeon. Her special interests include breast surgery, facial rejuvenation using liposuction, dermal fillers and Botox®, and body contouring using a variety of state-of-the art techniques. Dr. Ekstrom works with both Mentor Corporation and McGhan (Allergan) implant companies as an investigator using silicone implants in reconstruction and breast lift patients. She offers “quick recovery” breast augmentation and in-office small volume liposuction in which you save money by paying no hospital fees and no general anesthesia. Customized tummy tuck procedures are also one of her interests.

Watercolor painting, glass flame work, and photography are included in Dr. Ekstrom’s artistic interests, and she enjoys gardening and teaching financial and investment skills to women. Dr. Ekstrom raised two sons and a daughter, now grown, and resides in the central Massachusetts area.

Dr. Deborah Ekstrom

Education & Training

Dr. Ekstrom completed her undergraduate education at the University of Minnesota and graduated from the University of Minnesota Medical School in 1978. Her initial training in Minneapolis included surgery, micro surgery and psychiatry. In 1983, Dr. Ekstrom entered plastic surgery training at UMass Medical Center and became an associate professor. She then went into private practice with her special professional interests including face and body contouring.

Dr. Juris Bunkis, MD FACS

Double Board Certified Plastic Surgeon, Dr. Juris Bunkis, is the Medical Director and Owner of Orange County Plastic Surgery in both Newport Beach and Dublin, CA.

Dr. Juris Bunkis graduated from the University of Toronto Medical School in 1974. This was followed by a five year general surgery residency at Columbia University and board certification in general surgery, and a two year plastic surgery residency at Harvard University. Dr. Bunkis is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery in 1981, and is a Fellow of the American College of Surgeons. Dr. Bunkis is the Medical Director of Orange County Plastic Surgery in Newport Beach, CA. He joins Salisbury Plastic Surgery as an associate, on a part time basis, while maintaining his position in California.

Dr. Bunkis graduated from the University of Toronto Medical School in 1974. This was followed by a five year general surgery residency at Columbia University and board certification in general surgery, and a two year plastic surgery residency at Harvard University. Dr. Bunkis served as a Clinical Instructor in Surgery at Harvard University from 1979-81 and subsequently as Assistant Professor in Surgery, at the University of California, San Francisco. He is currently an Assistant Clinical Professor at the Massachusetts College of Pharmacy and Health Sciences. He has been in private aesthetic practice since 1983. Dr. Bunkis has also served for the past ten years as a voluntary Deputy with the San Bernardino County Sheriff’s Department and serves as the Honorary Consul for the Republic of Latvia in Southern California.

Dr. Juris Bunkis is an internationally known plastic surgeon, diplomat of the American Board of Plastic Surgery, and a fellow of the American College of Surgeons. Dr. Bunkis is a member of the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, American Society of Plastic Surgeons, Massachusetts Medical Society, Canadian Society for Aesthetic (Cosmetic) Plastic Surgery, Latvian Medical and Dental Association, Plastic Surgery Research Council and the California Society of Plastic Surgeons. Dr. Bunkis has published more than 40 scientific publications, abstracts and book reviews, and 17 book chapters in plastic surgery text books.

Dr. Bunkis specializes in facial rejuvenation procedures such as his Natural “A to V” Facelifts, brow lifts, eyelid surgery and nasal surgery (internal rhinoplasty without external scars). He has expertise in buttock, pectoral and calf implant surgery. He performs the full gamut of aesthetic procedures.
